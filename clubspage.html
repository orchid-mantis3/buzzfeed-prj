UCSD ACM - Association for Computing Machinery

Overview

UCSD ACM is a student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery, dedicated to fostering a community of computer science enthusiasts. We provide opportunities to learn, network, and grow through various programs and events.

Events and Programs

  • Workshops on programming, web development, and algorithms
  • Hackathons like HackUCSD
  • Networking events with industry professionals

How to Join

You can join UCSD ACM by signing up on our website or attending one of our events. Membership is open to all UCSD students interested in computing.

Meeting Schedule

General body meetings are held every Thursday at 6 PM in the Computer Science and Engineering building.

UCSD WIC - Women in Computing

Overview

UCSD WIC is an organization dedicated to empowering women and underrepresented groups in computing. We provide mentorship, resources, and a supportive community for personal and professional growth.

Events and Programs

  • Mentorship program connecting students with industry professionals
  • Technical workshops and coding bootcamps
  • Social events and community building activities

How to Join

Joining UCSD WIC is easy! Fill out the membership form on our website or attend our general meetings to get started.

Meeting Schedule

General body meetings are held every Wednesday at 5 PM in the Jacobs School of Engineering building.

ASL Club

Purpose

The ASL Club at UC San Diego strives to promote the use of American Sign Language while also spreading awareness of the culture of the Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing community. Our goal is to create a safe space for new and existing signers to expand their knowledge of ASL/Deaf culture and connect with other students who seek to advance in the language and understanding of the culture.

Strides Running Club

Purpose

We're a social, casual distance running club at UCSD for runners of all skill levels — from beginners who are just interested in giving it a try, to seasoned athletes training for big races! You'll almost always have someone to run with and you're guaranteed to make a few new friends. We also run in local races and host a variety of social events. Asian and Pacific Islander Student Alliance

Purpose

We, the students of University of California at San Diego (UCSD), come together to provide a forum to deal with the problems and concerns of UCSD Asian and Pacific-Islander (API) students. We aim to create an environment to address political, educational, cultural, and social issues and to increase awareness of our API history and heritage within the university and community.
